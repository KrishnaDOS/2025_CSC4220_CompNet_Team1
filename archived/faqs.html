Frequently Asked Questions

When will your website be fully functional?
Our website completion deadline has been postponed to April 21st, 2024.
Sorry for any inconvenience caused by the delay.


Where should I go to return my product?
Depending on your product specifications, you should either ship it to us,
or you should contact Support requesting us to transport your product.


Can I buy the same products in stores?
No. As stated in the About Us webpage, we do not sell these products in stores.


What should I do if my product is damaged?
An answer to this question has been given above.


What if my tractor/harvester encounters problems working?
All agricultural machinery sold has been CCC certified, so this should not be a problem.
If there are any problems, then please contact Support to either have your machinery
repaired free of cost (warranty lasts only 2 years for any machinery bought), exchanged
for a working one, or returned.
